What is an age calculator?

An age calculator is a simple tool that takes your date of birth and compares it with today’s date to find your exact age. Instead of counting only years, it breaks your age down into years, months, and days so you can see a more precise result.

How to calculate age online

Using this free online age calculator is very simple:

  • Enter your date of birth in the date field.
  • Click the “Calculate age” button.
  • See your age in years, months, and days.
  • Check how long is left until your next birthday.

Formula used in age calculation

The age calculation is based on the difference between today’s date and your date of birth. The tool:

  • Finds the year difference.
  • Adjusts months and days when the current date is earlier in the year.
  • Calculates how many days remain until your next birthday.
