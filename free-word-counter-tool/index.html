How this word counter works

The word counter updates in real time as you type. It splits text into words, sentences, and characters and uses an average reading speed to estimate how long it would take to read your content.

Word count for SEO and blogging

For blogging and SEO, word count can be an important factor. Many long-form articles perform well in search when they are clear, structured, and detailed. This tool helps you keep track of your length while you write.

Frequently asked questions

Does this tool store my text?

No. All processing happens in your browser only. Your text is not uploaded or saved on any server.

Is the reading time accurate?

Reading time is an estimate based on an average reading speed of around 200–250 words per minute. Actual reading time may vary between readers.
