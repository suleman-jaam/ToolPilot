How to calculate percentage

The basic percentage formula is very simple: percentage = (part / whole) × 100. Our calculator applies this formula automatically so you do not have to do the math manually.

Percentage formula explained

Here are some useful forms of the percentage formula:

  • What is X% of Y? → (X ÷ 100) × Y
  • Percentage increase → ((new − old) ÷ old) × 100
  • Percentage decrease → ((old − new) ÷ old) × 100

Examples

Some common examples:

  • What is 20% of 150? → 30
  • Your score increases from 60 to 75 → 25% increase.
  • A product drops from $100 to $80 → 20% discount.
